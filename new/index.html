About Modern Grantmaking

Our book is for anyone employed in the curious world of grantmaking, whether you work for a private foundation, a government funder, or a corporate donor.

We're written a practical guide that grantmakers can turn to whenever they need support. To make it quick and easy to use, we’ve filled the book with answers to real-world grantmaking questions.

But it’s about more than handy tips. Every grantmaker can be a reformer too - our book explains why this is so important and how you can get involved.

About the authors

Gemma Bull

Gemma is a nonprofit leader who was previously Funding Strategy Director for the UK's largest community funder, responsible for awarding £600 million annually. Previously she was Managing Director for Innovation at Leonard Cheshire Disability and Head of Innovation at Save the Chidren. She now specialises in supporting organisations to innovate, improve and collaborate.

Tom Steinberg

Tom is a nonprofit CEO and civic tech leader turned grantmaker. He founded civic tech pioneers mySociety before becoming a senior executive at the UK's largest heritage fund. Tom is an Ashoka Fellow, board member at ICIJ, and also co-directs a course at Harvard University.

Services we offer

Ever since we started writing a book for professional grantmakers, people from funders have been asking us if we provide services related to it.

We offer the following services for funding organisations, whether you work for a private foundation, government funder, or corporate donor.

  • The Modern Grantmaking Workshop: A full or half day workshop founded on the five values that underpin Modern Grantmaking. Each workshop is tailored to help grantmakers to surface and agree perhaps under-appreciated priorities for change in their organisations.
  • Embedding Modern Grantmaking: A longer engagement in which we will work closely with your team to identify areas where your organisation is performing strongly on Modern Grantmaking values and practices, and where some improvements could make a real impact, especially for grantees and grantseekers.
  • Redesigning your funding offer: We will work with you to review your current funding offer from start to finish using Human-Centred Design tools and techniques. The purpose is to ensure that your funding is more accessible and that the quality of applications you get are higher, with fewer confused or irrelevant applications.
  • Bespoke consulting: Modern Grantmaking, the book, covers a wide array of topics and areas. We’re happy to work with you on anything within the book, and where necessary will point you to others who can help you more than we can.

We are also available to book for events as keynote speakers or panellists.

If you’d like to talk to us about any of the services we provide we would be delighted to chat. Please drop us a line at hello@moderngrantmaking.com.
